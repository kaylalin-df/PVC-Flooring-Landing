Circular Process

The Lifecycle, Closed Into a Loop

Taiwan's PVC flooring recycling rate sits at 0% — a 30-year industry gap. Da Fon collects post-use PVC gloves and silicon wafers from tech fabs, crushes them and feeds the material back into LVT core layers. Each ping diverts 25 12-inch wafers + 50 pairs of gloves.

STEP 01

Manufacture

Recycled PVC and silicon-wafer powder enter the core layer; made-to-order by our strategic partner.

STEP 02

Install

Formaldehyde-free method with black underlay + pressure-sensitive adhesive; overnight install, next-day open.

STEP 03

Use

Each tile carries a circular-trace QR code — scan to view in-service time, location and tracking data.

STEP 04

Recycle & Remanufacture

Da Fon collects, crushes and feeds the material 100% back into the core of the next manufacturing batch.

Current Product Limitations — Disclosed

Honest disclosure builds long-term trust. Two known limitations to consider during evaluation.

PVC is not eligible for Taiwan's Green Building Material certification

This means the product is not eligible for tenders requiring Green Building Material status, while still meeting general ESG procurement and corporate sustainability standards.

Recycled content is currently around 10% (GRS international threshold ≥ 20%)

Our strategic manufacturer is actively optimising the formulation. Improvement is expected over the next three years as circular volume accumulates.
